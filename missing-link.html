The Missing Link

Fire-focused homeless and refugee advocates often muse that they should be in touch, but aren’t. Both communities know there’s a lot they don’t know. Both communities face needs in excess of their resources. But there’s little sharing or networking to show for it.

If you’re also interested in connecting homeless and refugee advocates on the fire issue, send an e-mail to kyle@pipecityfire.org.
